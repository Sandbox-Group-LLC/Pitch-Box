# Sandbox-XM

## What we are
An experience-design studio that builds **brand experiences and event-led growth programs**
for enterprise B2B teams. We treat the flagship moment — the council, the summit, the salon —
as **strategy, not staging**: audience-first thinking tied to pipeline outcomes.

## What we sell
- **Signature activation pitches** — concept-led owned moments (councils, executive dinners, architect labs, documentary series) built around a buyer's real pressure.
- **Event-led growth** — programs engineered so the room *is* the pipeline funnel: wired for signal capture → attribution → GTM handoff.
- **Borrowed-audience plays** — owned moments staged adjacent to the third-party shows a client's buyers already attend (control the guest list, borrow the crowd).

## How we're different
- We start from the **audience question**, not the floorplan.
- Every moment **ladders to meaning** and to a measurable outcome (pipeline, category capture, a booked roster).
- We're a **strategic partner**, not a production vendor — the opposite of "a product demo in a ballroom."
- We bring the **target-account-to-room map already built**, not a generic sponsorship deck.

## Proof / credentials
- Produced **Intel Vision, Las Vegas 2024**; Intel Federal Summit; Intel Event Content Review (Federal BU client).
- Track record convening enterprise rooms where the format is the strategy.

## Who we serve
Brand-led and GTM-led teams who believe experience is strategy — typically mid-market to
enterprise B2B, often around a flagship event, a category-positioning shift, a raise, or a
new marketing leader setting the agenda.

## The pitch model (how our concepts are built)
A **hero concept** (named) as the headline ask, with lighter pre-sketched shapes held as
negotiation leverage. Concept-led outbound (not cold capability decks). The room doubles as
the brand moment **and** the pipeline engine.
